Pivot & Trend Detection w/ Decay Methods

Overview

The Pivot & Trend Detection with Decay Methods indicator represents an advanced approach to identifying key reversal points using sophisticated mathematical weighting techniques. This system combines four distinct decay methodologies to provide comprehensive trend analysis with exceptional accuracy.

By employing weighted trend analysis through EMA, linear, exponential, and Gaussian decay methods, the indicator offers traders unprecedented insight into market momentum shifts and pivot point significance.

This indicator pioneers the use of multiple decay functions in pivot detection, providing more nuanced and accurate identification of significant market turning points.
