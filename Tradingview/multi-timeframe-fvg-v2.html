Multi-Timeframe FVG V2

Overview

The Multi-Timeframe FVG V2 indicator is a sophisticated Fair Value Gap detection system that simultaneously monitors three configurable timeframes. This advanced tool provides comprehensive market analysis by identifying price inefficiencies across multiple time horizons.

By combining gap detection across Higher Timeframe (HTF), Current Timeframe (CTF), and Lower Timeframe (LTF), traders gain unprecedented insight into market structure and institutional activity patterns.

Multi-Dimensional Analysis

This indicator revolutionizes FVG analysis by providing simultaneous multi-timeframe monitoring with dynamic visual filtering and automatic gap mitigation tracking.

Usage Guide

Setup and Configuration

Configure the indicator by setting appropriate timeframe multipliers based on your trading style. The system automatically adapts to your current chart timeframe and calculates HTF and LTF accordingly.

Trading Applications

Confluence Trading

Look for areas where multiple timeframe FVGs overlap to identify high-probability reversal or continuation zones.

Trend Confirmation

Use HTF gaps to confirm overall trend direction while using LTF gaps for precise entry timing.

Risk Management

Position stop losses beyond significant HTF gaps and use LTF gaps for profit-taking levels.

Interpretation Guidelines

  • HTF Gaps: Major institutional levels, strong support/resistance
  • CTF Gaps: Primary trading opportunities and structure breaks
  • LTF Gaps: Fine-tuning entries and exits, scalping opportunities
  • Overlapping Gaps: Confluence zones with higher probability
  • Mitigated Gaps: Completed institutional objectives
