Common Issues & Solutions

EA not opening trades

Check automated trading is enabled, verify symbol permissions, ensure sufficient margin, and confirm market hours.

High slippage on trades

Use market execution brokers, ensure stable internet connection, consider VPS hosting, and avoid news events.

Unexpected drawdown

Review risk parameters, check market conditions, verify strategy logic, and consider reducing position sizes.
