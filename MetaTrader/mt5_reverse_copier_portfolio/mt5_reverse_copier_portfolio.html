Executive Summary

The MT5 Reverse Trade Copier is a sophisticated automated trading system designed for professional traders requiring advanced risk management and hedging capabilities. This system monitors trades on a master MetaTrader 5 account and automatically executes reverse positions on a slave account, providing real-time portfolio protection and arbitrage opportunities.

Built with enterprise-grade reliability, the system features comprehensive error handling, real-time monitoring, configurable risk parameters, and detailed logging. It serves as a critical tool for traders managing multiple accounts, implementing hedging strategies, or conducting sophisticated risk management operations across different brokers or account types.

The system's intelligent reverse copying mechanism transforms every master account BUY order into a slave account SELL order and vice versa, enabling traders to maintain balanced exposure, test contrarian strategies, or implement advanced portfolio hedging techniques with minimal latency and maximum reliability.

Methodology & Core Logic

Real-time Trade Monitoring

The system continuously monitors the master account's deal history using MetaTrader 5's native API, detecting new position entries within seconds of execution. Advanced filtering ensures only relevant trade signals are processed.

Intelligent Reverse Logic

Each detected master trade triggers a sophisticated analysis process that determines the optimal reverse position parameters, including lot size calculation, symbol validation, and market condition assessment before execution.

Risk-Aware Execution

Before executing any reverse trade, the system validates account status, available margin, symbol availability, and market conditions. Configurable lot multipliers allow precise risk control and position sizing.

Comprehensive Error Management

Multi-layered error detection and recovery mechanisms ensure system stability. Real-time logging, automatic reconnection, and detailed error reporting provide operational transparency and reliability.

Risk Disclosure & Limitations

⚠️ Important Risk Information

Trading Risk: This system involves automated trading which carries substantial risk of loss. Reverse copying strategies may amplify market exposure and should only be used by experienced traders who understand the implications of maintaining opposite positions.

Technical Dependencies: System performance depends on stable internet connectivity, MetaTrader 5 platform availability, and broker server reliability. Network interruptions or platform issues may affect trade execution.

Market Conditions: The system performs optimally in normal market conditions. During extreme volatility, news events, or low liquidity periods, execution may be affected by slippage, requotes, or broker restrictions.

Account Requirements: Both master and slave accounts must have sufficient margin, enabled AutoTrading permissions, and compatible trading conditions. Account limitations or broker restrictions may impact system functionality.

Regulatory Compliance: Users are responsible for ensuring compliance with applicable financial regulations in their jurisdiction. Some regions may restrict automated trading or require specific licenses.
