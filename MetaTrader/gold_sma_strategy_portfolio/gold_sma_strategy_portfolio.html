Methodology & Logic

Conceptual Framework

The strategy employs a dual-SMA crossover approach enhanced with multiple validation layers. Unlike traditional crossover systems that wait for candle completion, this system detects crossovers as they happen and validates them through real-time price action analysis, ensuring faster market entry while maintaining signal quality.

Core Signal Generation Process

1
Real-Time Crossover Detection: Continuously monitors 7-period and 27-period SMA relationships, identifying bullish (short SMA crosses above long SMA) and bearish (short SMA crosses below long SMA) crossovers as they develop.
2
Candle Formation Analysis: Validates that the crossover candle is forming in the correct direction (bullish candle for buy signals, bearish for sell signals) with minimum body size requirement of 30 pips to ensure momentum.
3
Size and Quality Filters: Applies minimum candle size filter (30 pips total range) and body requirements to eliminate weak signals during low-volatility periods or market consolidation.
4
MA Touch Filter: Optional advanced filter that rejects entries if the entry price would be too close to either moving average line, avoiding potential whipsaw scenarios.
5
Breakout Execution: Executes trades when price breaks the high (for buys) or low (for sells) of the signal candle, confirming momentum continuation in the direction of the crossover.
